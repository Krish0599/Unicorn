Chewse
Chewse is a platform that allows companies to handle their food catering and workplace culture needs.
San Francisco, California, United States
Categories  E-Commerce, Hospitality, Personalization, RestaurantsHeadquarters Regions  San Francisco Bay Area, West Coast, Western USFounded Date Jul 1, 2011Founders  Jeff Schenck, Tracy LawrenceOperating Status ActiveFunding Status Late Stage VentureLast Funding Type Series CNumber of Employees 11-50Legal Name Chewse Inc.

Chewse takes a "Spotify for food" approach to office catering and provides companies with curated selections of family-style meals from local restaurants. Ordering food for offices is currently broken, and catering is a $55B industry in growth mode with 700,000+ enterprise customers feeding employees today in the US.

Chewse was founded in 2011 by Tracy Lawrence and Jeff Schenck and is based in San Francisco, serving both San Francisco and Los Angeles.
