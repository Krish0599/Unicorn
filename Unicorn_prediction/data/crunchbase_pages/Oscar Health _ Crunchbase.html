Oscar is a health insurance company that employs technology, design, and data to humanize health care. The company offers plans to individuals, couples, and families living in parts of New York, New Jersey, California, and Texas who don’t receive health insurance from an employer.

Founded in 2012, Oscar Health is headquartered in New York, New York, United States.
