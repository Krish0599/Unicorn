At StyleSeat, they're on a mission to help the world look and feel amazing by connecting anyone with the beauty services they need, anytime. They're honored to help beauty and wellness professionals in over 15,000 cities across the US manage and grow their business. Their online and mobile platform has created thousands of success stories by giving professionals everything they need to schedule online and showcase their work. Their nationwide directory features these websites, giving clients a place to search, discover, and book appointments, whether it's a same-day blowout, a haircut next month or a manicure tomorrow.
