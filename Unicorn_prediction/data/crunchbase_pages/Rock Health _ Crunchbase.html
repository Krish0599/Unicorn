Founded in 2010, Rock Health is the first seed fund focused exclusively on healthcare technology.

Rock Health provides entrepreneurs with funding ($250k), strategic and operational support, co-working space, and access to a top-tier network of partners, academic medical centers, and clinicians.

Wello

Benefitter
